What I do?

Here are some of my expertise

Economy Constructing

The super structure like footings, columns, beam, slab and staircase are small for all construction cost variants, the price difference of this economy construction cost is because of the finishing metrials used like bricks, m sand or robo sand used for building walls ans plastering, flooring materials wall and floor tiles for bathrooms and kitchen (Rs 25/sqft), doors and frames (Rs 5600 each) and window(Rs 10,500 each) and ventilators(Rs 1800 each)

Premium Constructing

The super structure like footings, columns, beam, slab and staircase are same for all construction cost variants, the price difference of this premium or high end construction cost is because of the finishing metrials used like bricks (rupees 9 to 11 per piece), river sand used for building walls ans plastering, flooring materials(100 to 120 per sqft) wall and floor tiles for bathrooms and kitchen (Rs 80 to 100/sqft), doors and frames (Rs 12500 each) and window(Rs 16,500 each) and ventilators(Rs 4800 each)

Luxury Constructing

The super structure like footings, columns, beam, slab and staircase are small for all construction cost variants, the price difference of this economy construction cost is because of the finishing metrials used like bricks(Rs 17 to Rs37/P), m sand or robo sand used for building walls ans plastering, flooring materials(Rs 450/sqft) wall and floor tiles for bathrooms and kitchen (Rs 185/sqft), doors and frames (Rs 25600 each), electrical cables and fixures like switches (Rs 185/sqft), sanitary ware like wash basin (Rs 6800/E), WC(Rs 22000/E).
